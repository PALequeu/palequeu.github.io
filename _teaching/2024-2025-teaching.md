**LU1IN001 - Introduction to Programming (1st-year bachelor)**
Introduction to programming and algorithmic, using a subset of python focused on education called [MrPython](https://docs.alias-asso.fr/mrpython.html)

**LU2IN009 - Introduction to Relational Databases (2nd-year bachelor)**
Introduction to databases, with a focus on SQL databases building and querying.

**EPU-N7-SPI - Industrial Project (1st-year master)**
Following a group of four students from Polytech Sorbonne Engineering school on their yearly project. They developped a great phone app to gamify waste sorting for kids.
