Publication following my Master's thesis internship at Fujistsu Luxembourg, under the supervision of Sofiane Lagraa and Moussa Ouedraogo.

We developped a simple composite metric based on the balance of protected attributes (gender, age, ethnicity, etc.) in tabular dataset, the Composite Balance Score (CBS), which correlates with bias in models after training on the data. We developped a web interface to evaluate any tabular dataset, re-balance it using different mitigation strategies, and train machine learning models and the balanced data.
