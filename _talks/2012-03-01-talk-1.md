Invited by the ParliView research team @ UC Dublin. 

Getting insights from large scale citizen consultations is not trivial. Existing methods strongly rely on the *Voting Matrix* of a consultation, where citizens vote that they "agree" or "disagree" on each other's propositions. However, this matrix is usually extremely sparse. algorithmically filling this matrix is a task known as Elicitation Inference Optimization (EIO). In this presentation, we explore different recommender system for this task.
